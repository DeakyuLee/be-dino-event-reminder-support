Frequently asked questions
How do I enable notifications for an event?
Tap any event on the home screen, then toggle "Notify me" on. You can also choose how many minutes before the event you want to be reminded.
I'm not receiving notifications — what should I check?
Make sure notifications are enabled for BeDino in your device Settings → Notifications. Also check that the event reminder is toggled on inside the app.
Why is the boss fight showing the wrong time?
The app uses UTC times for all events. Make sure your device timezone is set correctly in Settings → General → Date & Time with "Set Automatically" enabled.
How often does the event schedule update?
Event schedules are fetched from our servers each time you open the app. If the schedule looks wrong, close and reopen the app to get the latest data.
Is the app available on Android?
Yes — BeDino is available on both iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).
